FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

Car detailing is a thorough cleaning and restoration process that improves your vehicle’s appearance inside and out.

We recommend detailing every 3–6 months depending on usage and environmental conditions.

Minor scratches and light stains can often be removed through polishing and deep cleaning. Deep scratches may require paint correction.

A detailing session typically takes between 2 to 5 hours, depending on the vehicle size and selected service package.
